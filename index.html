Put pollution on the map

Garbage is accumulating in nature at an alarming rate everywhere, even in the most remote and pristine places. Accumulation of garbage and other types of pollution in nature is not only harmful to us but to all the living organisms that rely upon a clean environment for survival.

For environmental actions

The platform will be developed as a springboard for donating and crowfunding environmental and cleaning actions via an area system. This could allow schools and associations to generate revenues through meaningful actions.

Access on any device

The platform is compatible with all devices as a responsive webpage. We are also aiming at developing strategic partnerships to build embedded apps that allow marking and synchronizing marker mapped from wearables.
